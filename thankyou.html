Your test submission has been received successfully. Taking the first step to understand your mental health is a positive and important decision.

Mental health awareness is crucial for everyone. Remember that experiencing symptoms or concerns is normal, and seeking support is a sign of strength, not weakness.

We encourage you to:

  • Stay aware of your thoughts, emotions, and behaviors.
  • Reach out to trusted friends, family, or support groups if you feel stressed or overwhelmed.
  • Consult mental health professionals for guidance and care.
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle, including proper sleep, nutrition, and physical activity.
  • Use this website’s resources to learn more about common mental health conditions and coping strategies.

We hope this test has helped you better understand your mental health. Remember, these self-assessments are for awareness only—they are **not a diagnosis**. Professional evaluation is important for accurate support and treatment.

If you feel you need immediate help, please reach out to local mental health services or helplines.
